Courses

Courses For Brothers

Tafseer Course

No doubt the knowledge of Quran is the best of all knowledge and it is necessary for Muslims to not only learn the recitation of Holy Quran but also get to know the commandments and rulings of Sharia mentioned in it. Dawat-e-Islami has introduced a very beautiful Faizan-e-Tafseer course (Quran Interpretation) so that you get to know about the events of Prophets, bounties of Almighty and much more in a detailed manner.

Tasawuf Course

There is very much to learn from the lives of Sahaba, Tabieen, Taba Tabieen, and many Islamic saints who came after them. In Faizan-e-Tasawuf course you will learn about the mystic lifestyle of our pious predecessors which will further strengthen our faith and deeds which will make our bond strong with our Creator.

Fiqh Course

Bahar e Shariat a well-recognized religious book and this book is a source of becoming an Islamic scholar. In Faizan-e-Bahar e Shariat course you will learn detailed rulings regarding pillars of Islam like Salah (Namaz), Charity (Zakat) Fast (Roza), Pilgrimage (Hajj) and solutions of daily domestic life like Sunnah of Nikkah, Halal and Haram meat, Islamic rulings about new trade strategies of national and international level and much more.

Ahkam e Roza Course

Fasting is one of the sacred pillars of Islam and observed in the month of Ramadan every lunar year by every sane male and female Muslim. Ahkam e Roza Course will acknowledge you the importance of Ramzan and its fasts, conditions in which a fast will not be broken, who can give Kaffara in place of fast, Sunnah of Eid Salah, Sharia rulings regarding Fitrana and much other Islamic guidance related new matters to fasting like injection or drip.

Zakat Course

Zakat a very prominent pillar of Islam and it is obligatory upon those who reached the level of Nisab mentioned in Holy Quran. Zakat course will enable you to learn the conditions of Zakat being obligatory, sayings of Holy Prophet for those who don’t pay Zakah, who are zakat deserving person and modern-day problems and their solutions related to Zakat.

Seerat e Mustafa course

Seerat e Mustafa course will enlighten your heart and soul with the matchless biography of Holy Prophet. You will get to know the physical beautiful and unexampled appearance of Beloved Prophet, his attractive and polite way of talking; way he eats and drinks, dresses he wore and his sitting and standing position in daily practice and heart touching lifestyle.
